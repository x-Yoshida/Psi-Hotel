A lightweight and minimalist WordPress theme for Elementor site builder.

== Description ==

Hello Biz is a free, user-friendly Hybrid WordPress Theme that was crafted for seamless integration with the
Elementor site builder and tailored specifically for business websites.
Perfect for beginners, but far from limited to just them,
it features a dedicated beginner-oriented “Home” screen to simplify and streamline the web-building process.
Hello Biz also integrates with Elementor’s premium features, giving you access to tools like AI,
and accessibility enhancements in one place. Whether launching a startup site or refining a company portfolio,
Hello Biz offers a solid, responsive foundation for all web creators.

Report security bugs through the Patchstack Vulnerability Disclosure Program. The Patchstack team validates, triages, and handles vulnerabilities. [Report here](https://patchstack.com/database/wordpress/theme/hello-biz/vdp)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who Hello Biz Theme is For? =
>The Hello Biz Theme is designed to help you build professional business websites quickly and efficiently. It’s ideal for:
>- **SMB owners and entrepreneurs** from any niche, looking to create a professional business website with ease.
>- **Freelancers and professional Web Creators** who build websites for clients, enabling them to speed up website development, while maintaining a high level of customization.
>- **New professionals**, looking to start a website building and maintenance services business with a fast, intuitive, and scalable solution.

= Is Hello Biz suitable for beginners? =
>Absolutely! Hello Biz is designed for all skill levels, making it the perfect choice for beginners and professionals alike.
>- **Beginner-Friendly** – The step-by-step onboarding wizard makes setup effortless.
>- **Centralized Home Dashboard** – Easily access key sections of your website from one place.
>- **Flexible & Scalable** – While simple to use, Hello Biz also provides powerful customization options for experienced users looking to build advanced websites.

= Can I use Hello Biz to build sites for clients? =
>Yes! Hello Biz is an excellent choice for client projects.
>- **Versatile & Professional** – Designed specifically for business websites, making it ideal for client work.
>- **Time-Saving Features** – Pre-designed website kits and Hello+ Widgets help you build sites faster without compromising quality.

= Do I need to know how to design? =
>Not necessarily, Hello Biz is designed to make website creation simple and accessible—no design skills required.
>- **Intuitive Onboarding Wizard** – Follow the step by step guide to setup your website in just a few clicks.
>- **Professionally Designed Templates** – Choose from ready-made layouts tailored to various industries.
>- **Easy Customization** – Simply add your content, adjust colors and fonts, and your website is ready to go.

= Can I customize Hello Biz to fit my brand? =
>Absolutely! Hello Biz offers extensive customization options to ensure your website aligns perfectly with your brand.
>- **Intuitive Header & Footer Customization** – Easily adjust layouts, menus, and branding elements.
>- **Color Palettes & Typography** – Choose and apply your brand’s signature colors and fonts effortlessly.
>- **Simplified Styling Options** – Customize your site with user-friendly controls while maintaining a professional look.

= Are there any additional costs to using Hello Biz? =
>Hello Biz is a free theme available in the WordPress repository. You can enhance your website further by purchasing Elementor Pro, but it is not required for using Hello Biz.

= Can I upgrade from the Hello Theme to Hello Biz? =
>Yes, you can switch to Hello Biz without losing any of your work. It’s designed to integrate seamlessly with Elementor, so you can continue building and enhancing your site, while benefiting from the new powerful features and tools for a faster workflow.

= If I’m Already Using the Hello Theme, Do I Have to Switch to Hello Biz? =
>No, switching to Hello Biz is completely optional.
>- You can continue using the Hello Theme if it fits your workflow and project needs.
>- Switching to Hello Biz unlocks new features and Hello+ Widgets, making website creation faster and more efficient.

= How can I report security bugs? =
>Report security bugs through the Patchstack Vulnerability Disclosure Program. The Patchstack team validates, triages, and handles vulnerabilities. [Report a security vulnerability](https://patchstack.com/database/wordpress/theme/hello-biz/vdp)
